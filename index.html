Hackference is coming back!

Yes, that's right, Hackference will be back for a third year in a row.

We're still a single day conference with a full weekend hackathon, for developers by developers. But this time we're mixing things up a bit to provide you with even more awesome!

The idea behind Hackference is to inspire people to develop more for fun. Software and app development is a day job for most attendees, Hackference is all about finding more stuff to enjoy and have fun and hack with.

Call for Speakers

If you'd like to speak at Hackference, we'll be opening a Call for Speakers with the release of the site. If you'd like to find out more in advance, please e-mail cfp@hackference.co.uk
