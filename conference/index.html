The Speakers

We are lining up some fantastic speakers covering a fantastic array of topics, from different API's to alternative languages.

Snacks, Drinks & Lunch

You'll find unlimited coffee and tea, a lot of snacks and lunch at the conference. Plenty to keep you going during the day.

The Venue

We are at The Studio this year. The Studio is an amazing conference space - one of the best in Birmingham we think - and you'll be treated by its beautiful rooms, super location (perfect for those who are using public transport to get to us) and complimentary snacks, drinks and lunch.
